If I Redesign...

This is a side project that started off with the sudden thought of "What if I get to redesign some of the sites that I use almost daily?" Coz' while there are many sites that I like and often use, many are, frankly, not that interesting. There's Google.com, of course - simple, easy to use, but ultimately a little too boring for me. And then there's Amazon.com, which looks just a little too - what's the word? - stiff. There are more, all at the back of my mind and waiting to be redesigned.

Imaginative Design

So far, 'creativity' has been at the heart of web design and development, but I'd think the keyword from hereon should be Imagination, not creativity. The state of current frontend technologies, I think, is mature enough that we can stop thinking about simply coming up with designs, but of unleashing our imagination. Anything we can think of, we can achieveon the browser.

Google.com - As I Imagine It

Right now, though, I've only come up with my personal version of Google.com. (More coming up, tentatively with Amazon.com next in the queue.) Take a look at it, praise it, criticize it, or find out how I finally came up with this final design.
